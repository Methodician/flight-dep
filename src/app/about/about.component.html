Tricia was born with a forest green crayon in one hand and a keyboard and mouse on the other. From day one, she was ready to tackle the technological trends that would affect the design world. Making sure not to get tempted with the financial stability that comes with pursuing a career in the medical field, she stayed on track by achieving a Bachelors Degree in Illustration from the Cal State University of Long Beach. She plans to fulfill her childhood prophecy of furthering her claim in the design community by using shapes, lines, and composition in ways they thought they never could be used before. Watch out for her, as she is just getting warmed up.

Tricia is a Freelance Interaction Designer who is currently residing in the green-filled wonderland that is Portland, OR with her patiently supportive and encouraging significant other, Jacob, and their confidently introverted hamster Freddie Pepper who always graciously declines out-of-the-cage-time when the clock hits 7pm. When she is not busy fulfilling her prophecy, she enjoys hiking, drooling over the dribbble feed, and slaving away in the kitchen.
